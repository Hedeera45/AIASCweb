    <div align="center"><span class="page">A Brief Explication</span>
    </div>
    <p><em>Eye to the Telescope,</em> a quarterly online
            journal, began publishing science-fiction, fantasy, horror, and other
            speculative poetry in 2011, under the auspices of the <a href="http://sfpoetry.com/">Science
    Fiction &amp; Fantasy Poetry Association</a>. Its editors are selected by the current SFPA
    president and change with each issue; as a result, editorial policies
    change with each issue as well.</p>
      <p>Read our <a href="index.html">current issue</a> of  poems set in other
        worlds—some, as strange as this one. Peruse the labyrinthine <a href="archives.html">archives</a>        that
        shall form as our chronology accretes. When appropriate, <a href="submit.html">submit</a> your
        own genre poetry for the delectation of our editors, and—with their approval—our
        readership. See our <a href="https://sfpoetry.com/events.html" target="_blank">Events</a> calendar for submissions windows.</p>
      <p>Interested in editing an issue of <em>Eye to the Telescope</em>? See the <a href="editettt.html">Editors’ Guidelines</a> for information and requirements.</p>
      <p align="center"><strong><em>Forthcoming issues, editors, and themes:</em></strong></p>
